### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The workplace atmosphere is welcoming and open. Whenever I ran into issues, FCAJ members were always ready to support me, sometimes even after hours. The office itself is neat and cozy, making it easy to concentrate on my work. Moreover, the weekend social events where alumni share their journeys have been both inspiring and educational. The only minor drawback is that booking a desk to work in the office can be quite challenging.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
I received excellent support from my mentor and the team admin. The mentor gave me step-by-step guidance and always made me feel comfortable asking questions. Besides that, they frequently reminded me of key details and provided valuable information for my project.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The work was highly relevant to my major. My tasks connected well with my university studies but also exposed me to completely new technical areas. This balance helped me solidify my basics while building hands-on skills. Additionally, reading AWS documentation and using their real infrastructure gave me the chance to truly understand the concepts I had previously learned in class.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
During the internship, I learned many new skills, such as using project management tools, working effectively in a team, and communicating professionally in a corporate environment. FCAJ memebers also shared valuable real-world experiences that helped me better plan my career path. Cloud infrastructure is the backbone of modern technology, and I'm glad I had the chance to dive into one of the leading cloud platforms. This will definitely help me in my future studies and give me a strong advantage when applying for jobs.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
I really appreciate the company culture here. It is a great balance between serious work and a fun, respectful atmosphere. Whenever there was an urgent task, everyone stepped up to help each other out, regardless of their job title. This incredible team spirit made me feel completely welcomed and treated like a real team member from day one.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides an internship allowance and offers flexible working hours when needed. Additionally, the grading process is very fair and generous, creating a great environment that encourages personal creativity and new ideas.

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?
  The most satisfying part was being able to apply what I learned at university to real cloud projects. I also really appreciated how supportive everyone was. Working with such a friendly team and a dedicated mentor made my learning process much faster and more enjoyable.
- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
    I think the company could improve the desk-booking system. Since it is quite hard to register for a seat at the office right now, having a smoother or more guaranteed process for interns would make it easier for us to come in and connect with the team.
- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
Yes, I would, definitely. The working environment here is very open, the policies (like flexible hours and allowances) are great, and the mentors are extremely helpful. It is a fantastic place to build a solid technical foundation. Also, Cloud infrastructure is the future of the IT industry, so gaining hands-on experience here gives you a huge advantage for your career.

### Suggestions & Expectations
- Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?  
    I suggest improving the office desk booking system so that interns can come to the office more often to interact with the team. Additionally, having a short training session on basic AWS tools during the first week would help new interns adapt even faster.
- Would you like to continue this program in the future? 
    Yes, absolutely. I would love to participate in higher-level projects or continue working here in the future. This program is a great stepping stone for my career in cloud engineering.
- Any other comments (free sharing):
    I just want to express my deep gratitude to the entire FCAJ team. The weekend gatherings with former participants were truly inspiring. Thank you for creating such a supportive and wonderful internship experience!
